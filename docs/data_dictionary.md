# Data folder

This folder contains anonymized and synthetic CSV data for the Remote ERP Onboarding Workflow Case Study.

## Files

- `synthetic_onboarding_event_log.csv`  
  Reconstructed and anonymized event-level timeline of onboarding artifacts and activities.

- `artifact_inventory.csv`  
  Anonymized inventory of artifact categories observed during the case.

- `onboarding_failure_taxonomy.csv`  
  Failure taxonomy for remote ERP onboarding and knowledge-transfer breakdown.

- `role_expectation_matrix.csv`  
  Role modes and expectation risks observed in the onboarding structure.

- `knowledge_transfer_gap_matrix.csv`  
  Knowledge areas, prerequisites, observed artifacts, gap types, and recommended scaffolds.

## Important

These files do not contain raw customer documents, passwords, credentials, personal information, original meeting recordings, or confidential project files.

The data is reconstructed, anonymized, and summarized for research, portfolio, and CWI demonstration purposes.
